iCloud Trust Support

Setup help, license help, and troubleshooting for iCloud Trust.

  • Support email: leoappscontact@gmail.com
  • Typical response time: 1-2 business days
  • Platform: macOS 13 or later

Install

  • Download the DMG from your purchase email or release link.
  • Drag iCloud Trust.app to Applications.
  • Open the app and run your first scan.

License & Updates

  • iCloud Trust is a one-time lifetime purchase.
  • Updates are included at no extra cost.
  • If activation issues happen, email support with your order email and error screenshot.

What to include in support requests

  • macOS version
  • iCloud Trust version/build
  • What happened and what you expected
  • Screenshot or crash report (if available)
