EmberClip Support

Support for EmberClip on iOS, plus rollout updates for the upcoming macOS edition.

  • Support email: leoappscontact@gmail.com
  • Typical response time: 1-2 business days
  • Current platform: iOS 17 or later

Common questions

  • Clipboard capture on iOS works while the app is open and monitoring is enabled.
  • Open Settings in EmberClip to enable or pause automatic capture.
  • If paste prompts are frequent, set Paste from Other Apps to Allow in iOS Settings for EmberClip.

Include this in support requests

  • Device model and iOS version
  • EmberClip version and build number
  • What happened and what you expected
  • Screenshot or screen recording if available
