Privacy Policy for iCloud Trust

iCloud Trust is designed to analyze sync health while keeping your data exposure minimal.

  • iCloud Trust does not upload your file contents to Leo Dev Studio servers.
  • Diagnostics and scan analysis are performed locally on your Mac.
  • Proof exports are generated locally and saved only where you choose.
  • The app stores license state on your device (Keychain/UserDefaults) for activation continuity.
  • License verification uses Lemon Squeezy endpoints to validate purchase keys.
  • Payment processing is handled by Lemon Squeezy, subject to their own privacy terms.
  • No advertising SDKs are used.
  • Support contact: leoappscontact@gmail.com
