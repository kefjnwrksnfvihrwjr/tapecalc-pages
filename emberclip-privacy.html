Privacy Policy for EmberClip

EmberClip is built to keep clipboard data private and local to the device.

  • No account is required.
  • EmberClip does not sell your data.
  • Clipboard history is stored locally on your device.
  • No third-party ad SDKs are used.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are used.
  • Clipboard capture on iOS only occurs while the app is open and monitoring is enabled.
  • You can clear saved clipboard history at any time from within the app.
  • Uninstalling the app removes local app data from your device.
  • Support contact: leoappscontact@gmail.com
